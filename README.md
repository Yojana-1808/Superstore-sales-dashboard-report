# Superstore-sales-dashboard-report
This dataset represents monthly sales performance of a retail Superstore across different regions and product categories. The data is structured in Month–Year format, making it suitable for time-based analysis and trend visualization.

# Objevtives 
Analyze Monthly Sales Performance
To study sales trends over different months and identify growth or decline patterns.

Evaluate Region-wise Performance
To compare sales and profit across different regions (East, West, South, Central).

Category-wise Analysis
To determine which product category (Technology, Furniture, Office Supplies) generates the highest sales and profit.
